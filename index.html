A little about us

Our Story:

Our story began during the summer months of 2009 when both Chris and Jessica lived at La Mirage in Mission Valley. At the time, we lived on opposite sides of the apartment complex, yet our cars were assigned two stalls apart, so it was almost inevitable that we crossed paths and began talking.

As the saying goes, one thing led to another and before we knew it, we were on our first official date at the Miramar Air Show. This was memorable because the year prior, Jessica was Medical staff on the flight line aid station all weekend. When Chris and Jessica went together, they attended an event Chris had been given tickets to which involved front row seats on the flight line, catered meals and a staffed bar -- a far cry from the experience the year before and much more exciting!

The two have been inseparable ever since and eight years later, Chris proposed while visiting the Hawaiian Islands. Chris had been planning to propose for a while, but when they got to Waipi'o Valley on the Big Island of Hawaii, he couldn't imagine a more ideal place to ask Jessica for her hand in marriage. Conveniently, a message in a bottle with a marriage request washed ashore at the same time, so it was of course meant to be!

Bride & Groom:

He's a Business Administration and Management major managing the family business; she's a Doctor of Nursing Practice working as a Primary Care provider and Lieutenant Commander in the Navy Reserves serving as Officer in Charge of Headquarters Unit, Expeditionary Medical Facility Camp Pendleton. We both are adventuresome and enjoy outdoor activities. The Scripps Seaside Forum seemed fitting as we both love the natural beauty of the ocean and the appeal of a venue that is not overdone or too manicured so that the focus is a celebration with family and friends!

We look forward to our wedding and celebrating this joyous and momentous occasion with you!

Love, Chris and Jessie
