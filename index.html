A little about us

Our story began during the summer months of 2009. At the time, we were living near each other and would often cross paths as we went about our daily routine. As the saying goes, one thing led to another and before we knew it, we were on our first official date at the Miramar Air Show and have been inseparable ever since.

While visiting Waipi’o Valley on the Big Island of Hawaii, Chris asked Jessica for her hand in marriage. We look forward to our wedding and hope you are able to join us in celebration of this joyous and momentous occasion!
